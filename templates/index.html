Welcome to Workshops, the e-learning tool

About

This is Workshops, is a project created to manage student lists and educational content in an easy and adaptable way from low income public schools (with low resource computers) to large courses with many students in an organization, company or a college compatible with each other, built under a free software license in Django.

Features

  • Free and Open Source
  • Easy to use
  • Easy instalation
